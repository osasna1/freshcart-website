How FreshCart Market Works

From picking your store to getting groceries at your door — it's simple, fast, and reliable.

01
📲

Download the App

Get FreshCart Market on iOS or Android. Sign up in seconds with your name, email, and delivery address. No complicated setup — just download and go.

02
🏪

Choose Your Store

Browse from 8 partner stores: Walmart, No Frills, Metro, LCBO, Costco, FreshCo, Food Basics, and Dollarama. Each store shows its full product catalogue with real prices.

03
🛒

Add Items to Your Cart

Browse products, tap to add to your cart, and adjust quantities easily. You can shop from one store or mix and match across multiple stores in a single order.

04
💳

Checkout Securely

Pay your way — Apple Pay, credit/debit card, e-transfer, or cash on delivery. Review your order summary including the $5 delivery fee (free over $50) and $2.99 service fee before confirming.

Note: LCBO orders require age verification (19+) at the time of delivery. Please have valid ID ready.

05
📍

Track Your Order Live

Once your order is confirmed, watch it in real time on the map. You'll see when your shopper picks your items, when they leave the store, and exactly when they're at your door.

06
🚪

Receive Your Groceries

Your driver delivers right to your door. You'll get a push notification when your order is arriving. Contactless drop-off is available — just leave delivery instructions in the app.
