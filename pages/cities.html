LCBO Age Verification

Ordering from the LCBO requires age verification. You must be 19 years of age or older to purchase alcohol in Ontario. Our delivery driver will check your valid government-issued photo ID at the door. If you cannot provide valid ID, the alcohol items will not be delivered and a restocking fee may apply.
