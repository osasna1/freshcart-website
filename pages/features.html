App Features

Everything you need for a seamless grocery delivery experience — built right into FreshCart Market.

What's Inside

Packed with features you'll love

📍

Real-Time Order Tracking

Watch your order live on a map from the moment it's placed. See when your shopper picks your items, leaves the store, and arrives at your door. No more wondering where your groceries are.

🔔

Push Notifications

Get instant updates at every step — order confirmed, shopper assigned, items picked, out for delivery, and arrived. Stay informed without even opening the app.

🍎

Apple Pay Support

Checkout in one tap with Apple Pay. Fast, secure, and seamless — no need to type in card details every time you order.

💳

Multiple Payment Methods

Pay with Apple Pay, credit card, debit card, Interac e-Transfer, or cash on delivery. Choose what works best for you every single order.

🏪

8 Partner Stores

Shop from Walmart, No Frills, Metro, LCBO, Costco, FreshCo, Food Basics, and Dollarama — all in one app. No more switching between multiple delivery services.

🎉

First Order Free Delivery

New customers get their very first delivery completely free. No promo code needed — it's applied automatically when you place your first order.

🚗

Transparent Delivery Fees

Just $5 flat delivery fee. Orders over $50? Delivery is free. Plus a small $2.99 service fee — always shown clearly before you confirm your order.

🍷

LCBO Alcohol Delivery

Order beer, wine, and spirits from the LCBO delivered to your door. Age verification (19+) is required at delivery. Have your government-issued ID ready.

🏙️

3 Cities Covered

FreshCart Market currently delivers in Toronto, Oshawa, and Barrie — with more Ontario cities being added soon. We're growing fast.

🚪

Contactless Delivery

Prefer a no-contact drop-off? Add delivery instructions in the app and your driver will leave your groceries safely at your door.

⚡

Fast & Easy Checkout

Our streamlined checkout process gets you from cart to confirmed order in under a minute. Review your items, choose payment, and you're done.

🔒

Secure & Private

Your personal and payment information is always encrypted and protected. We never sell your data. Your privacy is our priority.
