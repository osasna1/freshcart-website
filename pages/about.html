About FreshCart Market

We're on a mission to make grocery shopping effortless for Canadians — one delivery at a time.

Our Story

Built for real people, real groceries.

FreshCart Market was founded with one simple idea: grocery shopping shouldn't be a chore. Whether you're a busy parent, a student, or just someone who'd rather spend their time doing something they love — FreshCart Market brings the store to your door.

We started in Oshawa, Ontario and quickly expanded to Toronto and Barrie. Our mission is to make fresh, affordable groceries accessible to everyone across Canada.

We partner with the stores you already trust — Walmart, No Frills, Metro, LCBO, Costco, FreshCo, Food Basics, and Dollarama — so you can shop with confidence every single time.
